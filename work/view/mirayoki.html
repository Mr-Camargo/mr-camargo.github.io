JavaScript 2020 - Now Open Source

Mirayoki is an open-source, JavaScript-based Discord bot that runs on Node.js using the Discord.js library.

When the COVID-19 lockdown was in place around 2020, the Mirayoki project was born. Since then, it has grown over time and became my first full-stack project that included having to learn to develop a complete server-side client on the 10th version of the Discord API, design a responsive website on pure HTML and CSS, learn about Heroku, and CI/CD integrations with GitHub actions.

Mirayoki is designed with communities in mind, whether they are a friend club or entire fandoms.

Highlights

Faster than sound.

Mirayoki goes over the slowness of other bots by serving requests faster than any other bot available.

It achieves to do so by replying to requested interactions only, saving time and resources.

A whole toolkit. With just a click.

Mirayoki possesses various functionalities that will enable your server to be secure, fun, and awesome.

To call any, you just have to click '/' to see all available commands.

Private by default.

Public information available to everyone in Discord is collected from the user.

Mirayoki does not infer a user's activity based on their interactions with Mirayoki, or the servers they are in.
